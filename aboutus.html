Our Story

Moonlight Brew Café was founded in 2015 by a coffee enthusiast, Keriech Ricketts, who had a dream of creating a space where people could connect over the perfect cup of coffee. What started as a small corner shop in Melbourne has now grown into a beloved community hub, known for its artisanal coffee, warm atmosphere, and commitment to sustainability.

Over the years, we’ve stayed true to our roots, sourcing the finest beans from ethical farms and crafting each cup with care. Our passion for coffee is matched only by our love for our customers, who inspire us every day to keep brewing and innovating.

Our Values & Goals

  • Quality: We use only the finest, ethically sourced ingredients.
  • Community: We believe in fostering connections and giving back to our community.
  • Sustainability: We’re committed to reducing our environmental footprint.
  • Customer Happiness: Your satisfaction is our top priority.
